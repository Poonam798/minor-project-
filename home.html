It is a platform that enables a company to easily and correctly allocate, track and grant leave as well as allow for employees to request and track their own leave.

It Eliminates paperwork, Removes manual interventions, Improves communication and also Offers real-time visibility of data.

A good leave management system is basically a process in which employees can apply for leave online and the employer can adjust various leave categories and adjust the leave policy to the company..

This process also allows employees to request leave, view the previous leave history, and the employer can review and approve leave requests and track the current leave.
